My name is Julian Triveri. I'm a undergrad at Purdue University and a full-time associate research software engineer at the Purdue Envision Center.

I have experience in real-time 3D art, basic animation, and high-level programming, and I'm currently expanding my skill-set to include graphics and low-level programming.

While I believe that form should always follow function, I still have a keen interest in design and aesthetics. I especially love creating refined visuals for performance-constrained platforms such as web and mobile VR. Working within such constraints requires a more human touch where problem solving and clever tricks make all the difference.
