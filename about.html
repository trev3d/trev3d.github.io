My name is Julian Triveri. I'm a undergrad at Purdue University and an employee at a university run visualization lab. Several projects shown on this site come from my work there.

While I believe that form should always follow function, I still have a keen interest in design and aesthetics. I especially love creating refined visuals for performace-constrained platforms such as web and mobile VR. Such constraints ask for more of a personal human touch to work within, and my ultimate goal is to make people ask "how did they do this with that?!"
