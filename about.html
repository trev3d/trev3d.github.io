about

I'm Julian Triveri, an undergrad at Purdue University and a full-time research software engineer at the Purdue Envision Center.

I have experience in real-time 3D art, basic animation, and scripting / high-level programming, and I'm currently exploring graphics and lower-level programming. I like wearing multiple hats and I'm always looking for new ones to try on.

I believe that form should always follow function, and I never want to compromise functionality for flashiness, but I still have a keen interest in design and aesthetics. I especially love creating refined visuals for performance-constrained platforms such as web and mobile VR. Working within such constraints requires a more human touch where problem solving and clever tricks make all the difference.
