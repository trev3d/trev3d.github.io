Projection-mapped motion graphics

Custom motion graphics projected onto a courthouse facade.

Thanks to Purdue Prof. Esteban Garcia Bravo, I had the opportunity to create two projection-mapped motion graphics for a local food & music festival.

I created these using Blender's parametric geometry and rendering tools together with a peer's bespoke base model of the courthouse . These animations played together with dozens of others on the facade of the Tippecanoe County Courthouse in Lafayette, IN.

Completed Summer 2022
